1982 to 2004: the road to the future

Bythe 1980s BMWhad lostnone ofits innovative prowess, turning outsuch forward-looking models as the BMWZ1 Roadster with its famous retractable doors, orthe 12-cylinder750i luxurysaloon. Italso unveiled interesting new concepts in the shape ofits 8 Series Coupés. Itis a tradition to which BMW remains faithful in the presentand beyond: in launching the new 6 Series orthe X3, the companyis fusing its own pastand future in characteristic BMW style.
